How to Play

Map

You start the game on the map. Work your way to the top of the map by completing different encounters. You must choose a path by clicking on the icons that are glowing red. Follow that path up to the boss to win.

Cards

You start with 12 cards in your deck. After each fight you will be rewarded with a choice of four cards to add to your deck. There are six card types that synergize with each other but also have their own unique playstyle.

Offensive Cards

Fire

Fire cards focus on inflicting burn which deals damage at the end of each turn.

Burn gets reduced by 1 each turn.

Fire kills enemies slowly over time by burning them to death.

Lightning

Lightning cards focus on dealing direct damage immediately with high cost cards.

Some cards can activate energize, which grants you more mana the following turn.

Gain a ton of mana, turn it into damage, and unleash it upon your enemies.

Negation Cards

Ice

Ice cards focus on inflicting frostbite which reduces buffs gained by 50%.

Some cards can help you gain block which helps negate damage.

Ice cards are best against enemies that try to gain buffs rather than attacking each turn.

Air

Air cards focus on inflicting windswept which reduces attack and burn damage by 50%.

Draw low cost cards like Winds of Change which gains power each time it's played.

Air cards are best against enemies that have high attack and burn damage.

Support Cards

Water

Water cards focus on healing.

Some cards activate blood siphon which heals you for 20% of the damage you deal.

Some cards activate regeneration which heals you at the end of each turn.

Earth

Earth cards focus on blocking and gaining thorns.

Block prevents damage by shielding your health.

Thorns deals damage back to attackers when they directly damage you with an attack.
